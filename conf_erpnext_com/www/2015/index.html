ERPNext Conference 2015

Friday, 9th October 2015; 10am–5pm @ Mumbai

The ERPNext Conference is a place where the users, developers and community around ERPNext meets and discusses the way forward.

Over the last couple of years, ERPNext has established itself as one of the most exciting Open Source Projects in the Open Source Business Apps space. With the user and developer community growing every day, this is the ERPNext Conference is the place to be if you want to be involved in the future of business apps.

Read about the past ERPNext Conference

Who Should Attend

  • Users and potential users of ERPNext: Learn about how you can maximize value out of this amazing platform. Find out what other users are doing and show off your guru status by sharing your tips. Network and learn about service providers on the platform.
  • ERPNext Developers: Understand what opportunities lie ahead for you and network with service providers and potential users.
  • Open Source Enthusiasts: Hear the story of one of the legends of Open Source software and learn about one of the most successful open source projects from India.

Speakers

Jordan Hubbard
CTO iX Systems and co-founder of the FreeBSD Project
Before joining iX Systems, Jordan was the Director of Unix at Apple and is responsible for putting FreeBSD on hundreds of millions of Apple devices across the world. iX Systems is an ERPNext user and Jordan will share his journey from founding the FreeBSD project to working with one of the most iconic companies of our era.
Rushabh Mehta
Founder of ERPNext and the Frappe Framework
Rushabh will share some of the latest developments by the ERPNext team and the roadmap for putting ERPNext at the heart of thousands of business across the world.
Sukh Dugal
Founder of the design firm Studio March
Studio March is responsible for the amazing look of the ERPNext and Frappe Framework. Sukh will talk about how businesses will transform themselves in the coming generation with apps and solutions that do not exist today.
Users
Key ERPNext users will talk about their experience using ERPNext and share some tips and ideas on what worked for them and how business can use a product like ERPNext to take their organizations to the next level.
Developers
ERPNext developers will showcase some of the apps they have built on the Frappe platform including connectors to 3rd party applications, Business Intelligence tools and other apps.
