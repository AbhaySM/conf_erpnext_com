Speaking at the conference

Key ERPNext users will share their experiences, tips and ideas for taking your business planning online. Developers will showcase the apps they've built on the Frappé platform.

Jordan Hubbard

CTO iX Systems and Co-fouder of the FreeBSD Project

Before joining iX Systems, Jordan was the Director of Unix at Apple, putting FreeBSD on millions of devices. Jordan will share his journey from founding the FreeBSD project to working with one of the most iconic companies of our era.

Rushabh Mehta

Founder of ERPNext and the Frappé Framework

Rushabh will share some of the latest developments by the ERPNext team and the roadmap for putting ERPNext at the heart of thousands of businesses across the world.

Abhay Singh

Designer at Studio March

Studio March is responsible for the new look and feel of ERPNext and the Frappé Framework. Abhay will talk about the thought and creative process behind the first design-focused update to ERPNext.

Who should attend

ERPNext users, current and future, learn what others are doing, share tips and network. Developers, understand what opportunities lie ahead and meet your users. Open source enthusiasts, hear the story of one of the legends of open source software.
